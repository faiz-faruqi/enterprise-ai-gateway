# Nordex Energy Equipment Supply Agreement

## 1. Parties

This Equipment Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Nordex Energy GmbH
("Supplier") and Meridian Power Holdings Ltd ("Buyer"), effective 15 March 2025.

## 2. Contract Value and Payment Schedule

The total contract value for equipment supply, installation supervision, and commissioning
support is €3,750,000 (three million seven hundred and fifty thousand euros), structured as:

- 20% advance payment upon contract signing: €750,000
- 40% upon delivery of equipment to site: €1,500,000
- 30% upon mechanical completion and commissioning: €1,125,000
- 10% retention, released upon performance testing sign-off: €375,000

All payments are subject to Value Added Tax at the applicable rate.

## 3. Scope of Supply

The Supplier shall design, manufacture, test, and deliver:

- 12 × NTW 3.6 MW onshore wind turbine generators with hub height 120m
- Tower sections, nacelle components, and rotor assemblies
- SCADA monitoring system with 10-year data retention capability
- Commissioning and technical advisory services (28 days on-site)

## 4. Term and Delivery Schedule

The initial delivery window is 18 months from contract signing. Milestone delivery dates:

- Foundation interface drawings: 60 days from signing
- Equipment delivery to port: Month 12
- On-site delivery and erection support: Month 14–16
- Commissioning completion: Month 18

## 5. Termination Conditions

Either party may terminate this Agreement:

- For material breach not remedied within 45 days of written notice.
- For insolvency or appointment of an administrator by either party.
- By the Buyer for convenience with 120 days written notice, subject to a termination
  fee equal to 15% of the undelivered contract value at the time of notice.

The Supplier may suspend delivery if any payment is overdue by more than 30 days and
written notice has been issued. Suspension does not constitute termination.

## 6. Performance Guarantees

The Supplier warrants a minimum annual energy yield of 97.5% of the design estimate over
the first 24 months of operation, subject to availability of wind resource within the
contracted P50 range.

Liquidated damages for underperformance: €18,000 per percentage point below the guaranteed
yield, capped at 10% of the total contract value.

## 7. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond
reasonable control, including war, acts of government, pandemic, extreme weather events
(defined as events exceeding 1-in-50-year recurrence intervals), or supply chain
disruptions affecting raw material availability for more than 60 consecutive days.

The affected party must notify the other in writing within 5 business days of the event.
Force majeure events extending beyond 180 days entitle either party to terminate without
penalty.

## 8. Data Protection and Cybersecurity

The SCADA monitoring system shall comply with IEC 62443 cybersecurity standards. All
operational data transmitted to the Supplier's remote monitoring centre shall be encrypted
in transit (TLS 1.3 minimum) and at rest (AES-256).

The Supplier shall not share operational performance data with any third party without
prior written consent from the Buyer.

## 9. Intellectual Property

All designs, engineering drawings, and technical documentation provided by the Supplier
remain the intellectual property of Nordex Energy GmbH. The Buyer receives a perpetual,
non-transferable licence to use documentation solely for operation and maintenance of
the installed equipment.

## 10. Liability Cap

The Supplier's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed 100% of
the total contract value. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or
loss of revenue damages except in cases of wilful misconduct or fraud.

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Disputes shall be referred
to the London Court of International Arbitration (LCIA).
