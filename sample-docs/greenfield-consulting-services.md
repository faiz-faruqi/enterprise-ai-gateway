# Greenfield Consulting Services Agreement

## 1. Parties

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Greenfield
Advisory Partners LLP ("Consultant") and Meridian Power Holdings Ltd ("Client"),
effective 1 February 2025.

## 2. Contract Value

The total fee for the engagement is £480,000 over 12 months, payable monthly in arrears
at £40,000 per calendar month. Time and materials work beyond the agreed scope is billed
at £350 per hour for Principal Consultants and £220 per hour for Senior Analysts.

## 3. Services and Deliverables

The Consultant shall provide:

- Regulatory strategy advisory for the Client's offshore wind licensing programme
- Stakeholder engagement planning and execution across BEIS, Ofgem, and NatureScot
- Quarterly board-level briefings (written report + presentation)
- Regulatory change monitoring service with fortnightly bulletins
- Representation at up to 8 external regulatory hearings per year

Key deliverables by quarter:
- Q1: Regulatory landscape assessment and risk register
- Q2: Stakeholder engagement strategy and consultation responses
- Q3: Licence application supporting documentation
- Q4: Post-submission regulatory management plan

## 4. Term and Renewal

The initial term is 12 months from the effective date. Either party may renew for
successive 6-month periods by providing written notice at least 60 days before expiry.

## 5. Termination Conditions

Either party may terminate this Agreement:

- For convenience with 60 days written notice.
- For material breach not remedied within 21 days of written notice.
- By the Client immediately if the Consultant's key personnel depart and suitable
  replacement is not agreed within 15 business days.

Upon termination, the Client shall pay all fees accrued to the date of termination
plus any pre-approved expenses incurred.

## 6. Intellectual Property Ownership

All written deliverables, reports, presentations, and analysis produced specifically for
the Client under this Agreement and fully paid for are assigned to the Client on receipt
of final payment.

The Consultant retains ownership of all pre-existing methodologies, frameworks, templates,
and proprietary tools used to produce the deliverables. The Client receives a non-exclusive
licence to use such underlying materials solely in connection with the deliverables.

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to treat all non-public information received from the other party as
strictly confidential for the duration of the Agreement and for 3 years thereafter.

The Consultant shall not use Client data, project details, or strategic plans for any
purpose other than delivering services under this Agreement.

## 8. Non-Solicitation

For 12 months following the termination of this Agreement, neither party shall directly
solicit for employment any member of the other party's core team who was materially
involved in the delivery of services.

## 9. Performance Milestones

Consultant performance shall be reviewed quarterly against agreed KPIs:
- Deliverable completion: 95% of agreed outputs delivered on schedule
- Stakeholder meeting attendance: minimum 90% of booked sessions
- Response time: written responses to Client queries within 2 business days

The Client may withhold 10% of the monthly fee if two consecutive quarterly reviews
show material KPI underperformance, pending remediation.

## 10. Liability

The Consultant's total aggregate liability shall not exceed the total fees paid in the
6 months preceding any claim. The Consultant maintains professional indemnity insurance
of £5,000,000 per occurrence throughout the engagement.

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Disputes shall be resolved
through the Courts of England and Wales.
