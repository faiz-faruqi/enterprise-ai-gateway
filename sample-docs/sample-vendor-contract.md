# Sample Vendor Contract — Acme Software Services

## 1. Parties

This agreement is entered into between Acme Software Services Ltd ("Vendor")
and Enterprise Corp ("Client"), effective 1 January 2025.

## 2. Contract Value

The total contract value for the initial term is £240,000 (two hundred and forty
thousand pounds), payable in quarterly instalments of £60,000.

## 3. Term and Renewal

The initial term of this agreement is 24 months commencing on the effective date.
The agreement will automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless
either party provides written notice of termination at least 90 days prior to
the end of the then-current term.

## 4. Termination Conditions

Either party may terminate this agreement:

- For convenience, with 90 days written notice to the other party.
- For material breach, with 30 days written notice if the breach is not remedied
  within that period.
- Immediately, in the event of insolvency or cessation of business by either party.

## 5. Vendor Responsibilities

The Vendor shall:

- Deliver the software platform as specified in Schedule A.
- Provide 99.5% uptime SLA during business hours (08:00–18:00 GMT, Monday–Friday).
- Respond to Priority 1 incidents within 1 hour.
- Assign a dedicated Customer Success Manager for the duration of the contract.
- Supply monthly usage and performance reports by the 5th business day of each month.

## 6. Data Protection

The Vendor shall process all Client data in accordance with applicable data
protection legislation including UK GDPR. The Vendor shall not transfer Client
data outside the United Kingdom without prior written consent from the Client.

All data processed under this agreement shall be deleted within 30 days of
contract termination.

## 7. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property created solely by the Vendor in the course of delivering
services under this agreement remains the property of the Vendor. Any work
product created specifically for the Client and funded by the Client shall be
assigned to the Client upon full payment.

## 8. Liability

The total liability of either party under this agreement shall not exceed the
total fees paid in the 12 months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.
